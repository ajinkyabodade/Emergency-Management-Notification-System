About EMNS Service

EMNS provides an emergency alert system for users for reporting of community technological emergencies. The user reports the incident using our proposed smart phone application with the Aadhar ID and the particular event description. The information is kept confidential, and is only used to keep track of the user in case of an emergency, or alert reporting. The authorities are then able to confirm the occurrence of the emergency, and notify the users within that region to take specific action, if required.

Also EMNS can use this database to send various notifications for power cut, water supply, taxes and various update about accidents, to the peoples in that specific region.

It can be useful at places where it becomes difficult to manage to convey the disaster message in short time. It can also be useful for huge area and to implementation in large area will result in less damage . It also reduces time interval between receiving the disaster information and implementation for it. It can be also helpful for the women’s security in critical situation.
